Kuya’s Beach: A Peaceful Seaside Escape in MacArthur, Leyte
Located along the coastline of MacArthur, Leyte, Kuya’s Beach is a serene and charming destination that offers visitors a tranquil seaside experience. Known for its quiet atmosphere, scenic ocean views, and uncrowded shoreline, this beach is a favorite local retreat—perfect for travelers seeking peace, simplicity, and a connection with nature. Seaside Serenity with a View
Unlike more commercialized beaches, Kuya’s Beach offers a laid-back, rustic coastal environment where the sound of waves replaces the noise of city life. The soft stretch of sand, gently lapping waves, and a backdrop of coconut trees make it an ideal location to unwind, read a book, or simply sit and watch the tide roll in. The beach faces the Leyte Gulf, providing beautiful sunrises and a soft sea breeze throughout the day. Early mornings and late afternoons are particularly magical, with golden light reflecting across the water and casting a warm glow over the entire shoreline. Perfect for Relaxation and Simple Beachside Activities
Kuya’s Beach isn’t about luxury—it’s about authentic beachside enjoyment. It invites visitors to embrace the basics: barefoot walks along the sand, impromptu picnics under the shade, or casual dips in the cool sea. Popular activities include:

  • Swimming in calm, shallow waters
  • Beach games like volleyball or frisbee
  • Shell collecting along the tide line
  • Family picnics or simple barbecue cookouts
  • Napping in a hammock under coconut trees
Visitors will find ample open space to stretch out and enjoy the quiet. The beach is also suitable for families, couples, and small groups looking for a private and peaceful seaside escape.

A Local Favorite with a Welcoming Vibe

Kuya’s Beach is well-loved by locals from MacArthur and nearby towns, not only for its calm waters but for the friendly, welcoming atmosphere. It is often used for family outings, community gatherings, and weekend relaxation. You might see small fishing boats offshore or neighbors sharing food along the beach—adding to its homegrown charm. Occasionally, small cottages or huts may be available for day-use rental, depending on local arrangements. The simplicity of the beach is part of what makes it so appealing—no loud crowds, no big resorts—just the sea, the breeze, and the sand.
