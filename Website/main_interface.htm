MACARTHUR'S BEACHES

LANAWAN

Known for its lush landscapes and tranquil ambiance, it's an ideal spot for those seeking relaxation and a connection with nature. It has farm-to-table dining, high-value crops & flower gardens, outdoor activities, and a peaceful retreat.

LAKE BITO

Lake Bito is a serene freshwater lake located in Barangay Villa Imelda, MacArthur, Leyte, Philippines. Covering approximately 126 hectares, it is surrounded by rice fields and mountains, offering a picturesque landscape that reflects the region's natural beauty. ​

KUYA'S BEACH

Known for its tranquil atmosphere and scenic views, it's an ideal spot for relaxation and beachside enjoyment

OCEAN BREEZE

A serene seaside destination located in Barangay San Pedro, MacArthur, Leyte, Philippines. This resort offers a tranquil environment ideal for relaxation and unwinding.​

BAGAKAY FESTIVAL

A vibrant cultural celebration held annually on June 17 in MacArthur, Leyte, Philippines. It commemorates the town's founding anniversary and pays homage to its historical roots.
